What We Updated This Week
  • Masters Campaign expanded — 15 new targeted ad sets launched Monday May 11, covering a broader range of qualified search terms. Already approved by Google and actively firing. Goal: expand reach beyond tightly defined keywords and capture both current applicants and future pipeline.
  • PhD Campaign expanded to 11 ad sets using lower search-volume keywords that are more cost-effective and less competitive. Primary goal for PhD: reduce cost per conversion (vs. Masters goal of higher impressions).
  • Google image policy update resolved — ads requiring a text box offset from the image have been updated. No impact on campaign performance.
  • Negative keyword added: "fully funded PhD programs" — not accurate to ICSW’s offering, removed to improve relevance and reduce wasted spend.
